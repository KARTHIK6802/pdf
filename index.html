Why Plant Leaf Diseases Identification Important?

Detection of plant disease through automatic technique is beneficial as it ,

  • Reduces a large work of monitoring in big farms of crops.
  • It detects the symptoms of diseases.
  • It is cost effective.

The timely identification and early prevention of crop diseases are essential for improving production. In this website, deep convolutional-neural-network (CNN) models are implemented to identify and diagnose diseases in plants from their leaves, since CNNs have achieved impressive results in the field of machine vision
